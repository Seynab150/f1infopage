Our top five Romance books

The Tea Planter’s Wife by Dinah Jefferies is our fith place book, it is a wonderful story about a young women who travels to Ceylon to join her husband on his tea plantation but there is more than meets the eye and a lot of secrets. A beautifully written story that captures the reader.

Eleanor & Park by Rainbow Rowell is our fouth place book, it is a heart-warming story that follows two characters who couldn’t be more different that fall for each other. A young adult/teen romance book with some very special characters.

Me Before You by Jojo Moyes is our thrid place book, it is a story that follows an ordinary girl who takes on a new job that changes her life. She meets Will Traynor who is wheelchair bound after an accident. A beautiful love story with a captivating plot that will break your heart.

The Notebook by Nicholas Sparks is our second place book, it is a beautiful story set in North Carolina that follows Noah and Ally. Two people who met many years ago and spent a beautiful summer together and just couldn’t let each other go.

The book that takes the number one spot is Pride And Prejudice by Jane Austen, this romantic story follows Elizabeth Bennet and her lover Mr Darcy. It is a descriptive story that is brilliantly written.

Our personal monthly pick is It Ends With Us by Colleen Hoover, Lily hasn't always had it easy, so when she feels a spark with a gorgeous neurosurgeon named Ryle Kincaid, everything in Lily's life suddenly seems almost too good to be true.
